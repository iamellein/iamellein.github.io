An Insider’s Guide to NYFW

NYFW can be both amazingly fun & incredibly overwhelming. Especially if you’ve never been. Luckily, I’m here to give you an insider’s guide and make your first show a pleasurable experience.

Getting Tickets & Picking the Shows

If you’re lucky or connected, you can get an invite sans the price tag. But I wasn’t so lucky or connected my first 2 years. I’m here to help you plan your schedule, set a budget, and prioritize the shows you want to see.

Dressing for the Shows

Always stay true to your own sense of style. NYFW is about expressing yourself while appreciating the designers' new lines. Also, wear comfortable shoes, as you’ll be on your feet all day!
